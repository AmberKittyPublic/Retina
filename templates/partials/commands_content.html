Moderation

/ban <user> [reason]Permanently ban a userBAN_MEMBERS
/kick <user> [reason]Kick a userKICK_MEMBERS
/warn <user> <reason>Warn a user (stored in database)MODERATE_MEMBERS
/warnings <user>View a user's warning historyNone
/mute <user> <minutes> [reason]Timeout a user (max 28 days)MODERATE_MEMBERS
/purge <count>Bulk delete messages (2–100)MANAGE_MESSAGES
/slowmode <seconds>Set channel slowmode (0 to disable, max 21600)MANAGE_CHANNELS
/lockdown <lock|unlock>Lock or unlock the current channelMANAGE_CHANNELS

Scheduling

/tempban <user> <minutes> [reason]Temporarily ban a user (auto-unban after duration)BAN_MEMBERS
/tempmute <user> <minutes> [reason]Temporarily mute a user (max 28 days)MODERATE_MEMBERS
/scheduled announce <channel> <title> <message> <datetime> [interval]Schedule a one-time or repeating announcementMANAGE_GUILD
/scheduled listList all pending scheduled actions and announcementsMANAGE_GUILD
/scheduled cancel <id>Cancel a scheduled item (use A# or S# from list)MANAGE_GUILD

Giveaways

/giveaway reroll <message_id> [count]Pick new winner(s) for a ended giveawayMANAGE_GUILD
/giveaway end <message_id>Force-end a giveaway earlyMANAGE_GUILD
